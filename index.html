Planets

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and the closest to the Sun. It’s super hot during the day and freezing at night!

Venus

Venus is Earth’s “sister planet” but much hotter. Its thick clouds trap heat, making it the hottest planet.

Earth

Earth is the only planet known to support life. It has the perfect conditions for living, with air, water, and land.

Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet because of its rusty color. Scientists think it may have had water in the past.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the biggest planet. It has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot that’s been raging for hundreds of years!

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and dust. It’s a gas giant, just like Jupiter.

Uranus

Uranus is a chilly, icy planet that spins on its side. It’s one of the coldest planets in the solar system.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and has strong winds. It looks blue because of its thick, icy atmosphere.

fun space facts

The Sun is a Big Star!

The Sun is just a really big star! It’s so huge you could fit 1 million Earths inside it.

Space is Super Quiet

In space, it’s as quiet as a mouse because there’s no air for sound to travel through!

Jupiter Has LOTS of Moons

Jupiter has 95 moons and counting! One of them, Ganymede, is even bigger than the planet Mercury.

A Day on Venus is Sooo Long

A single day on Venus is longer than a whole year on Earth! Imagine having your birthday every day there.

Stars Can Turn Into Diamonds!

There’s a star in space nicknamed “Lucy” that’s made of diamond. It’s the biggest diamond ever found!
